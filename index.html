Our Policies

Refund & Dispute Policy

At Cozy Homes LLC, we are committed to fair and transparent business practices. Our refund and dispute policy includes:

  • Full refund of security deposits within 30 days of move-out, less any documented damages
  • Clear documentation of all charges and fees
  • Formal dispute resolution process through our customer service department
  • Mediation services available for unresolved disputes

Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances can change. Our cancellation policy includes:

  • 30-day notice required for lease termination
  • Early termination fees may apply as per lease agreement
  • Military deployment orders exempt from early termination fees

Terms and Conditions

By engaging with our services, you agree to:

  • Comply with all lease terms and community guidelines
  • Maintain the property in good condition
  • Pay rent and fees according to the agreed schedule
